Welcome to Worm Game!

Here are the power-ups and their effects:

  • Speed Up: Speeds up the worm for 5 seconds. (Blue)
  • Double Points: Doubles the points for food eaten for 10 seconds. (Orange)
  • Break Power-Up: If you have this power up, you will not die if the worm runs into itself, but you will lose the body below the point of collision. (Yellow)
